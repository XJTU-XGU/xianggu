Biography

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Information Science, School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Zongben Xu and co-supervised by Prof. Jian Sun. I got my B.S. degree in the School of Mathematics in June 2017 from the School of Mathematics, Southeast University.

Research Interests

My research interests include optimal transport, domain adaptation/generalization, and their applications in computer vision.
