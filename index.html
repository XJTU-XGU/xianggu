Biography

I'm an assistant professor at the Department of Information Science, School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University. I got my PhD degree in Applied Mathematics in March 2024, from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Zongben Xu and co-supervised by Prof. Jian Sun. Before that, I got my B.S. in Statistics in June 2017 from the School of Mathematics, Southeast University.

Research Interests

My research interests include optimal transport theory and algorithms, transfer learning in statistics and AI, and applications in biology and science. Currently, I am co-supervising students with Profs. Zongben Xu and Jian Sun.

非常欢迎自驱性强的本科生和研究生，课题组提供优良的研究条件，特别欢迎大三、大四本科生加入团队进行科研训练并力争发表顶会/顶刊论文。
