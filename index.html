Biography

I'm an assistant professor at the Department of Information Science, School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University. I got my PhD degree in Applied Mathematics in 2024, from the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Zongben Xu and co-supervised by Prof. Jian Sun. Before that, I got my B.S. in Statistics in 2017 from the School of Mathematics, Southeast University.

Research Interests

My research interests include optimal transport theory and algorithms, transfer learning in statistics and AI, and applications in biology and science. Currently, I am co-supervising students with Profs. Zongben Xu and Jian Sun.
