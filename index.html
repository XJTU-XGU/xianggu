Biography

I'm a fourth year PhD student of Department of Information Science, School of Mathematics and Statistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Zongben Xu and co-supervised by Prof. Jian Sun. I got my B.S. degree in School of Mathematics in June 2017 from School of Mathematics, Southeast University.

Research Interests

My research interests include optimal transport, domain adaptation/generalization and their applications in computer vision.
